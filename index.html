About SAB Holdings

Founded in 2007, SAB Holdings grew from a single F&B concept into a diversified holding company with expertise in creating and scaling restaurant and retail brands across Egypt and KSA. We combine branding, operations, supply chain and marketing to deliver consistent growth.

Why SAB?

  • Proven track record: 18+ years in F&B with multiple successful brands.
  • Scalable footprint: 48+ stores and 2,000+ employees across Egypt & KSA.
  • Operational strength: end-to-end expertise in branding, operations and supply chain.
  • High growth potential: clear roadmap to expand regionally.

Portfolio Overview

Our portfolio spans quick-service concepts, premium seasonal experiences and fast-casual formats. We support brands with centralized ops, supply chain, marketing and creative services to scale efficiently.
